Mystery plant care

Sunlight Full sun
Season to plant Late Winter
Water requirements Low
Maintainance Low
Soil type Moist, but well drained
Average lifespan 2-4 Years

How to Plant

  1. Fill a medium pot with a mulch-earth mixture
  2. Lightly cover 5-10 seeds with earth
  3. Keep moist until 5cm tall, then move outside to greenhouse

How to take care of your plant

  • This plant will grow well in both cool and warm temperatures, but will die back in scorching heat
  • The plants will grow best in direct sunlight, and might die back/shrink if left in the dark for long periods of time
  • Giving the plant nutrients from sources like compostevery now and then will help it grow, but it will gro fine without
  • They are very resilient and are only damaged/killed by root rot from overwatering or heavy/clay soil
  • The plant will grow wide, so eventually it should be divided

Other information

  • It is not toxic
  • You can keep growing both plants after dividing
  • This plant can be left outside all year, and is often planted directly into the ground when big enough
  • Some Gardeners prefer to cut it's flowers off, but that is only an aesthetic choice
